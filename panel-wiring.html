Panel Wiring

The Panel Wiring project is to wire the circuit in a panel according to a schematic diagram. The circuit is to control a water pumping station. I have learned to understand to read a schematic diagram and install and use devices such as programmable relay, din rails, terminal blocks, push buttons, two-way switch, pilot lights and power supply. I have also learned to number the wires in a tidy manner.
